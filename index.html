Introduction

"Start by doing what's necessary; then do what's possible; and suddenly you are doing the impossible." - Saint Francis of Assisi

EtBonum is a tool for entrepreneurs to develop their ideas.

The name EtBonum comes from the Fransiscan expression "Pax et Bonum" (i.e. "Peace and Good") which is the traditional greeting of the Fransiscan friars.

As a tool, it offers a way to outline both the concept and practicalities with a focus on the human dimension.
